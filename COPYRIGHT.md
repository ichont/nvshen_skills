## Why the upstream notice remains

This is the legally safer approach for a rewritten open-source derivative:

- keeping the upstream MIT notice preserves attribution
- keeping it avoids making a false claim that all preexisting material is exclusively owned by the current maintainer
- new copyright can coexist for newly added original material
